About Me

Hi! My name is Joann, and I'm from Taiwan!

Currently a master student in Music Technology at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Former BM student in Music Performance (Violin) at the Butler School of Music (studied under Prof. Sandy Yamamoto) at UT Austin and a merit scholarship recipient from the school.

I was a research assistant working with Dr. Yi-Hsuan Yang at the Music and AI Lab at Academia Sinica.

My research interests are at Music Information Retrieval, Symbolic Representation, and Emotion Recognition.
I also "try" to maintain a music review blog, check it out at JJ's Music Diary!

There's always more to discover in music.
